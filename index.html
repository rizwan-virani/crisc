Train for the CRISC.
Walk in exam-ready.

A comprehensive, self-paced, open-source training ground for the ISACA CRISC certification. In-depth coverage of all four exam domains, 400 Rapid Recall flashcards on a spaced-repetition scheduler, a 400-question bank, risk-decision simulators plus a drag-and-drop Technical Taxonomy Mapping engine, 20 hands-on sandbox labs, a full-length timed mock exam, and an Industry Certification Explorer.

Authored & designed by Professor Rizwan Virani

How to use this platform

  1. Read the Exam Mechanics & Career Guidance cards first. Understand the high-stakes landscape: the fixed-form 150-question format, the 200–800 scoring scale, the IT risk-practitioner mindset, and where CRISC sits in an IT-risk, GRC, and governance career.
  2. Deep-dive the four Domain Study cards. Each opens a rigorous, scenario-driven reading interface that mirrors a comprehensive prep manual, from IT risk governance and assessment to risk response, reporting, and the technology it lives in.
  3. Lock in the terms with Rapid Recall Flashcards. 100 cards per domain plus a Master Acronym Drill, powered by spaced repetition — grade each card honestly and the scheduler resurfaces what you're forgetting and retires what you know.
  4. Validate your knowledge with the Domain Quizzes. Ten focused questions per domain, drawn at random and graded with full rationale.
  5. Train practical judgment in the PBQ Simulators. 30 interactive performance-based questions across IT risk governance, risk assessment, risk response and reporting, and technology and security, plus a drag-and-drop technical taxonomy mapping engine that has you sort real-world scenarios into the correct categories.
  6. Benchmark readiness with the full-length, timed Mock Exam. 150 questions weighted exactly to the official domain percentages, with a countdown timer and a scoring dashboard.
  7. Get hands-on in the Labs. Twenty interactive sandbox labs spanning every exam domain — flip each card to review its objectives, then launch a full-screen console with live risk-decision walkthroughs, configuration panels, and a SYS_ADMIN log.
  8. Plan your path in the Supplemental Learning Hub. Open the Industry Certification Explorer to compare 39 industry certifications across 12 vendors, with costs, exam blueprints, DoD 8140 mapping, and salary and role insights.

Notice: This platform is optimized for desktop or laptop computers running the Google Chrome browser. Interactive elements, including the hands-on labs, terminal simulations, and drag-and-drop activities, require a mouse and keyboard to function correctly. These features may not display or operate properly on mobile devices.

What this is

  • An academic preparation resource that tests your knowledge dynamically with original, randomized questions and hands-on simulators.
  • A comprehensive study portal covering every objective in the official ISACA CRISC exam content outline.
  • Free, open-source, and self-paced. Everything saves to your browser — come back where you left off.

What this is not

  • Not a source of actual or copyrighted ISACA exam questions, "brain dumps," or leaked items. Using real exam content violates the ISACA Candidate Agreement and Code of Professional Ethics.
  • Not an official ISACA product and not affiliated with or endorsed by ISACA.
  • Not a guarantee of a passing score. It is a practice tool — the exam tests judgment, not memorization.
